## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analytical review of the privacy policies of major Saudi government applications, including **Absher**, **Sehaty**, and **Tawakkalna**. The study evaluates how these platforms align with the **Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)** and the guidelines set by the **Saudi Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority (SDAIA)** to ensure user data privacy and ethical data management.

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- **Policy Evaluation:** Analyzing the transparency and clarity of privacy policies in government apps.
- **Legal Compliance:** Assessing adherence to the National Data Governance Framework and PDPL.
- **Professional Responsibility:** Exploring the ethical implications of data collection and user consent in the digital era.

## 🔍 Methodology
The analysis follows a structured approach:
1. **Background Research:** Reviewing SDAIA regulations and the evolution of data protection in KSA.
2. **Comparative Analysis:** Benchmarking the privacy features of Sehaty, Absher, and Tawakkalna against national standards.
3. **Problem Identification:** Highlighting potential areas for improvement in user data transparency.
